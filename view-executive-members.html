Shri P.K. Bansal, after completing M.A. (Sanskrit) and LL.B., joined the Indian Police Service in 1963. Over his distinguished career in Gujarat, he served as Superintendent of Police, Commissioner of Police, Head of State Intelligence, and Director General of Police. For his long and meritorious service, he was awarded the Police Medal by the President of India in 1995.
Since retiring in 1997, he has remained dedicated to public service, especially helping the poor and common citizens in police matters. Humble, approachable, and deeply social, he has been serving as President of APCA for 17 years, driving its growth into a highly active and respected social organization. He has also been unanimously elected as President of Gujarat Pradesh Agrawal Mahasabha, guiding and uniting the Agrawal community across the state.
