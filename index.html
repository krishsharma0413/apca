Welcome to APCA

Agrawal Professionals Cultural Association (APCA), Ahmedabad, Gujarat. An Initiative by Agrawal Professionals….since 1992

Objective:

To promote socio – cultural interaction amongst the Professional Agrawal community in Ahmedabad with a view to contribute towards the up-lifment of Agrawal Community, in particular and the society at large.

We believe in the power of community and the strength that comes from professionals supporting each other in their personal and professional journeys.

President's Message

Dear Members,

You are aware that APCA has completed most active and eventful 20 years after it was started in year 1992 by some Agrawal officers of ISRO. It has grown tremendously since then and is well known as a most active and elite Agrawal Samaj in Gujarat.

You will be happy to know that APCA has started its own website

"www.agrawalprofessionals.com"

which will be highly useful and informative to all members of APCA and YUVA APCA. The site will give you details about all Executive Committee Members as well as other APCA and YUVA APCA members, you will also get all circulars about our future programmes, details of plans of activities, photographs of activities held, important events – current & future and many more. I am sure members will make full use of our website.

P.K. Bansal

President
