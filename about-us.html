About APCA

Agrawal Professionals Cultural Association (APCA) 1992

Jai Shree Agrasen Maharaj


APCA was founded in 1992 by a few Agrawal Scientists and officers of Indian Space Research Organisation at Ahmedabad. It has grown tremendously since then and has encompassed Agrawal Professionals from different fields outside ISRO.

Now it is a body of senior officers from central and state Governments and Public Sector undertakings as well as professionals from diverse fields and disciplines. APCA members include senior officers from I.A.S., I.P.S., ISRO, ONGC, PRL, Income Tax, Customs & Excise, Railways, Banks and professionals like Engineers, Doctors, Scientists, Chartered Accountants, Company Secretaries, Advocates, Professors, etc.

APCA is a vibrant Association and is known all over the State for its activities in social, cultural, educational, and philanthropic fields.

YUVA APCA

APCA started its youth chapter by the name "YUVA AGRAWAL PROFESSIONALS
CULTURAL ASSOCIATION" for young Agrawal professionals in the age group of 21 to 35 years, in the year 2006. YUVA APCA has also grown continuously. They join APCA activities like Agrasen Jayanti and Annual Sports Meet and also organize their separate activities during the year.

The Constitution of APCA has undergone some changes in the past. A copy of the current Constitution is included in the souvenir. List of present APCA and YUVA-APCA members are also included to facilitate interaction amongst the members. The contribution of previous Executive Committees must be cognized in the growth of APCA and therefore lists of the Executive Committee during the last 20 years are also included.

APCA Activities & Events

  • Annual functions with family participation
  • Children's talent development programs
  • Games and sports events in January
  • Public speaking contests
  • Treasure Hunt events
  • Annual functions with family participation
  • Educational lectures on various topics
  • Excursion trips to historical places
  • Foreign tours (e.g., Sri Lanka)
  • Quarterly newsletter publication

APCA is regularly publishing quarterly newsletters for the last 12 years, which include details of important events and activities, birthdays and marriage anniversaries, promotions, transfers, achievements and lists of meritorious students who are awarded silver medals and certificates on the occasion of the Agrasen Jayanti every year. Matrimonial ads given by APCA members are published free of cost.

APCA is having its own website and email id. Directory of APCA and Yuva-APCA members have been published in the past giving details of member, spouse and children. The same has been appreciated and found useful by all members.
